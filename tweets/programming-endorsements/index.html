Common Lisp contra-endorsements (2) ⚓

CL newbs: STAY AWAY from "Let over Lambda", at least until you've acquired proper CL taste, lest it teach you bad habits and warp your mind.

— Jean-PhilippeParadis (@HexstreamSoft) July 23, 2012

CL newbs: Beware of Paul Graham's CL coding style, it's not considered "idiomatic" by many (myself included). Example: Prefers DO over LOOP.

— Jean-PhilippeParadis (@HexstreamSoft) July 24, 2012
