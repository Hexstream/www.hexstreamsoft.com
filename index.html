My heartfelt recommendations ⚓

  • Quickdocs: A very promising new documentation and search engine site for all projects in Quicklisp!
  • SBCL: By far the most popular CL implementation. It's Open Source (mostly Public Domain), reliable, very fast, and well-supported by its maintainers and library authors.
  • Quicklisp: The One True Way to get CL libraries. Period!
  • quicklisp-projects issues: Great way to keep tabs on Quicklisp inclusion requests and such.
  • cl-test-grid reports: Got a library in Quicklisp? Definitely check the associated reports for success/failure across multiple CL implementations and platforms.
  • Programming in the twenty-first century: One of the best programming blogs on the planet! A very high ratio of "homeruns".
  • The Surprising History of Copyright and The Promise of a Post-Copyright World: If you read one single article about copyright in your entire life, this should be the one.
  • @CodeWisdom: Tons of great programming quotes. Extremely high-quality twitter feed!
