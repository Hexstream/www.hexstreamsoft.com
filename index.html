News ⚓

  1. 12 september 2015

    I finally decided to publish something that was rotting on a not-actually-private branch:
    the incomplete "full reference" part of my "Common Lisp FORMAT reference" article.

    I made some very minor updates to help orient people better, as well.

    You should check out my fairly comprehensive FORMAT summary table first, if you haven't yet.

  2. 13 january 2015

    I was always a bit puzzled that the CLHS does not cross-reference its figures, so now here they are all in one place, with machine-readable versions for most of them.

    See the tiny announcement for a few more details.

  3. 27 october 2014

    I just published a fairly major update to my "Notes and tips: Standard Common Lisp symbols" article.

    I did quite a lot of cross-referencing of the symbols to their various categories, thanks to an Emacs Lisp script. It's not fully comprehensive yet but I contend that finding related standard Common Lisp symbols has never been easier! This should be a boon to newbies in particular.
